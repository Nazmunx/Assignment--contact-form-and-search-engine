What is anant?

Bangladesh's first and full-fledged search engines

Ant is the first and only search engine in Bangladesh capable of working in both Bengali and English. This open web service helps to search for information across the country. It automatically analyzes and stores news, Bangla blogs, Bangla Wikipedia and government information from the country's major Bengali and English newspapers. Efforts have been made to focus on analyzing and searching Bengali data in Pipilika.
